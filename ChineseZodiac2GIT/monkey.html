Monkey

People born in the year of the Monkey are active, energetic, positive, and good listeners. They love to have fun and being stimulated. People born in the year of the monkey are bad at long-term relationships and suffer from weaker morals. They are compatible with people born in a year of the Dragon or Rat.

Recent years of the Monkey: 1968, 1980, 1992, 2004, 2016.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the monkey is very prosperous. They will be very successful in terms of acquiring money. Their health will be excellent most of the year. However, love and marriage will be very stale.
