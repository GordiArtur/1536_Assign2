Tiger

People born in the year of the Tiger are charming, courageous, intense, driven, and good leaders. Just like real tigers they are always ready to take action instantly in a situation. They are also moody, self-centered, and can be authoritative. They are most compatible with people born in a year of the Horse or Dog.

Recent years of the Tiger: 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the Tiger is not good, and the world will feel like it is against them. They will encounter people who will try to take advantage of them this year. As long as they remain open-minded they should be able to find opportunities somewhere.
