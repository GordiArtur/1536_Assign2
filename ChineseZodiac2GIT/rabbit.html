Rabbit

People born in the year of the Rabbit are compassionate, popular, and sincere. They also enjoy time spend with friends and family, and hosting people at their home. Because they avoid conflict, some people think of them as easy to push around. They are most compatible with people born in a year of the Goat or Pig.

Recent years of the Rabbit: 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2025.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the Rabbit is on the negative side. The year will see a lot of obstacles to overcome and it will be difficult to get anywhere in terms of career or financially situation. They need to focus extra hard on communication to overcome these set backs in 2016.
