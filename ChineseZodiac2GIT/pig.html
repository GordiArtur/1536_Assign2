Pig

People born in the year of the Pig are tasteful, polite, intelligent, nice, and perfectionists. They get a lot of value and enjoyment out of helping others. People born in the year of the Pig can be dangerous if you lie to or sabotage them. They are compatible with people born in a year of the Goat or Rabbit.

Recent and future years of the Pig: 1971, 1983, 1995, 2007, 2019.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the pig is negative. Their career, health, money-situation, and personal relationships will all be at an all-time low. Hopefully they can bounce back from this in 2017.
