Dragon

People born in the year of the Dragon are charismatic, egotistic, warm-hearted, and energetic. They are great at giving commands and will do whatever it takes to be in charge. People born in the year of the Dragon make some of the finest leaders. They are most compatible with people born in a year of the Rat or Monkey.

Recent years of the Dragon: 1964, 1976, 1988, 2000, 2012, 2024.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the Dragon is positive. This should be a very prosperous year for them. If they work hard this year, any small misfortune can become a huge positive in their life.
