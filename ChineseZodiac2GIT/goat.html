Goat

People born in the year of the goat are wanderers, thinkers, creative, insecure, and unorganized. They enjoy spending most of their time alone and thinking to themselves. People born in the year of the goat can have anxiety problems and need lots of reassurance. They are compatible with people born in a year of the Rabbit or Pig.

Recent years of the Goat: 1967, 1979, 1991, 2003, 2015, 2027.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the goat is very positive. All of the large setbacks they will face will be turned into positives. There will also be an overwhelming feeling of being content this year. Their career will flourish and they will discover many opportunities to make money.
