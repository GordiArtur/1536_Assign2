Dog

People born in the year of the Dog are faithful, loyal, sensitive, honest, and dogmatic. They are often caught telling small lies and can suffer from large changes in mood. People born in the year of the Dog are usually good at business. However, they can struggle with relationships in their personal life. They are compatible with people born in a year of the Horse or Tiger.

Recent and future years of the dog: 1970, 1982, 1994, 2006, 2018.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the Dog will be average. They will be successful in their career but face trouble overcoming challenges. It is vital that they stay true to themselves and remain calm this year.
