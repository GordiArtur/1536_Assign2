Rooster

People born in the year of the Rooster are observant, analytical, practical, resourceful and to the point. They are also often neat, conservative, honest, trusting, and focus on perfection. They are compatible with people born in a year of the Snake or Ox.

Recent and future years of the Rooster: 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, 2029.

The 2016 fortune for those born in the year of the Rooster will be positive and negative. Their career will see improvement this year; however, they may find it difficult to see opportunities to make money. Overall their health will be good in 2016.
